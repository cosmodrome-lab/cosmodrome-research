# COSMODROME
## Forensic Intelligence for On-Chain Governance

---

## The Problem

Most institutional analysis in DeFi is built on declared intent:

- Governance forums
- Team statements
- Token models as designed

But capital is exposed to something else:

> *What is actually deployed. Who actually controls it. How that control changes over time.*

These are not the same.

---

## What COSMODROME Does

COSMODROME produces forensic case studies of DeFi protocols — focused on control, not narrative.

Each case answers three questions:

1. **Who has authority?** — contracts, upgrade keys, execution paths
2. **Where does economic pressure accumulate?** — emissions vs. absorption, treasury flows
3. **How does behavior evolve?** — governance activity, coordination patterns, anomalies

---

## What You Receive

Each dossier includes:

- A structured risk map of governance and control surfaces
- A fixed multi-factor assessment model (10 indicators)
- A fully referenced evidence appendix (hash + timestamp)
- A plain-language risk narrative for decision-making
- Cross-protocol comparables within our case registry

---

## What Makes This Different

**Code Over Narrative** — We do not use forum posts or team claims as evidence.

**Absence Is a Signal** — Missing or removed data is treated as a risk event, not a gap.

**Point-in-Time Accuracy** — Each report is tied to a verifiable on-chain and repository state.

**Non-Reproducible Dataset Layer** — Select indicators are derived from internally maintained data not reconstructible from public sources alone.
